Vehicle Rental System

A powerful Java-based solution for managing your vehicle fleet.

Manage Cars, Bikes, and Trucks with Ease

Our Vehicle Rental System provides a user-friendly graphical interface to handle all your rental business needs. Track inventory, manage rentals, and keep records persistent.

Download JAR (v1.0)

Requires Java Development Kit (JDK) 8 or higher.

Key Features

Fleet Management

Easily add new vehicles (Cars, Bikes, Trucks) to your fleet database.

Rent & Return

Streamlined process for renting out vehicles and processing returns.

Smart Search

Quickly find vehicles by their unique ID to check status and details.

Data Persistence

All data is automatically saved to a local file, ensuring no records are lost.
